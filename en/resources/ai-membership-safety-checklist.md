## The three rules

1. **Identify the purchasing account and billing channel before paying.** Web, App Store, Google Play and third-party orders are managed separately.
2. **Share only the minimum information required for the confirmed flow.** Never share passwords, verification codes or recovery codes.
3. **Verify the plan on the official product page.** A bank authorization, receipt and active entitlement are different states.

## Product-specific data boundaries

| Product | Minimum data that may be involved | Never provide | Verify on |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ChatGPT | ChongGrok's automated Plus flow uses a sensitive session credential for that upgrade | Password, email code, recovery code | ChatGPT plan settings; sign out and back in afterwards to refresh the session |
| Grok | Grok User ID identifies the target account; it is not a login password | X/Grok password, codes, recovery data | grok.com or the original subscription channel |
| Claude | ChongGrok may use a Claude User ID to identify the target account; this is not an Anthropic billing requirement | Password, codes, recovery data | Claude settings or Billing |
| Gemini | Only the user's own account is supported; required information is confirmed case by case | Google password, two-step code, recovery code | Gemini, Google One and the original billing channel |

## Before payment

- Confirm the account can sign in and is owned by the intended user.
- Check for an existing plan, trial, family membership or pending order.
- Use the current official plan page for names, prices, limits and regional availability.
- Record whether billing is handled by the product website, Apple, Google Play or an independent provider.
- Ask who handles cancellation, refunds and entitlement support.

## After payment

| Status | What it proves | What it does not prove |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Pending bank charge or authorization | A payment request reached the bank | The merchant captured it or the plan is active |
| Receipt or completed order | The billing provider recorded a transaction | The currently signed-in account owns that order |
| Active App Store or Google Play subscription | The store manages an active subscription | The app is signed in to the purchasing product account |
| Target plan shown by the official product | The current account recognizes the entitlement | That future renewals cannot fail |

## If the entitlement is missing

1. Stop and do not pay again.
2. Save the order ID, exact time, billing channel and transaction status.
3. Confirm the original purchasing account and sign-in method.
4. Check the subscription in the original billing channel.
5. Reopen or update the official app, then sign in to the correct account.
6. Contact the billing provider for incomplete charges, the product provider for missing entitlements, or ChongGrok for a ChongGrok order.

## Evidence to keep

- Product, plan and term;
- billing channel and purchasing account;
- receipt, order ID, time and transaction status;
- masked account identifier;
- official plan screen or exact error message;
- troubleshooting steps already attempted.

Do not post complete session credentials, User IDs, email addresses, order numbers, card details or verification codes in public forums or screenshots.
